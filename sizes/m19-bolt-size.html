M19 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

What is an M19 bolt?

An M19 bolt has 19 mm nominal diameter. Treat it as a specialty fastener and confirm thread pitch and class with gauges or drawings.

M19 is rare in general hardware; sourcing taps, dies, and mates may require industrial suppliers. Follow OEM documentation. Spanish reference: perno M19.

FAQ

What is the standard pitch for an M19 bolt?

The common coarse pitch for M19 is 2.5 mm. See drawings for fine or special pitches.

What drill size should be used for tapping M19 threads?

For M19 x 2.5 coarse thread, use approximately a 16.5 mm tap drill. Use our Tap Drill Calculator for other pitches.

What imperial fastener is closest to M19?

3/4-10 (approx.) is a practical reference for comparing M19 to inch hardware; verify fit for your application.
