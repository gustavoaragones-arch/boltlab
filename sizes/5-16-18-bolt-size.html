5/16-18 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

5/16-18 is a common imperial (UNC) bolt size. A 5/16 inch nominal diameter with 18 threads per inch is used in automotive, machinery, and structural applications in North America.

Common uses

5/16-18 bolts are used in automotive, machinery, and structural connections. They are a standard size between 1/4-20 and 3/8-16 and are widely stocked.

FAQ

What does 5/16-18 mean?

5/16 is the nominal diameter in inches (0.3125 in). 18 is the number of threads per inch (TPI). It is a common UNC coarse thread.

What drill size for 5/16-18 tap?

Use an F drill (0.257 in) for a standard 5/16-18 tap.

What metric bolt is closest to 5/16-18?

M8 is the closest metric equivalent to 5/16-18. See M8 bolt size for full specs.
