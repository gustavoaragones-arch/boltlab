M20 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

What is an M20 bolt?

An M20 bolt has 20 mm nominal diameter with 2.5 mm coarse pitch in common ISO listings. Large structural bolts may use different pitches per project standards.

M20 is standard in steel construction and large machines for anchor-style and high-strength joints. Match structural specifications for grade and torque. Spanish reference: perno M20.

FAQ

What is the standard pitch for an M20 bolt?

The common coarse pitch for M20 is 2.5 mm. Fine pitch options include 1.5 mm or 2.0 mm where specified.

What drill size should be used for tapping M20 threads?

For M20 x 2.5 coarse thread, use approximately a 17.5 mm tap drill. Use our Tap Drill Calculator for other pitches.

What imperial fastener is closest to M20?

3/4-10 is a practical reference for comparing M20 to inch hardware; verify fit for your application.
