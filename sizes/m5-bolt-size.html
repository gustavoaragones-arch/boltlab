M5 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

M5 is a popular metric size for machinery, automotive, and general hardware. With a 5 mm nominal diameter and 0.8 mm pitch, it fits between M4 and M6 in strength and availability.

Common uses

M5 bolts are used in machinery, automotive components, electronics, furniture, and structural applications where a medium-small fastener is required.

FAQ

What is the standard pitch for an M5 bolt?

The standard pitch for M5 is 0.8 mm.

What drill size should be used for tapping M5 threads?

For M5 x 0.8 thread, use a 4.2 mm tap drill.

What imperial screw is closest to M5?

#10-24 is the closest imperial equivalent to M5.
