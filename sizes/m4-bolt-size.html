M4 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

M4 is a common small metric fastener used in machinery, automotive trim, electronics, and general hardware. A 4 mm nominal diameter with 0.7 mm pitch offers a good balance of strength and compact size.

FAQ

What is the standard pitch for an M4 bolt?

The standard pitch for M4 is 0.7 mm.

What drill size should be used for tapping M4 threads?

For M4 x 0.7 thread, use a 3.3 mm tap drill.

What imperial screw is closest to M4?

#8-32 is the closest imperial equivalent to M4.
