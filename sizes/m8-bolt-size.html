M8 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

Quick answer

M8 bolts use 8mm major diameter with 1.25mm coarse pitch and a 6.8mm tap drill.

They are standard for medium-duty machinery and structural brackets.

Use the chart below for hex and drill data, then metric to imperial conversion for stocked UNC parts.

Metric vs UNC thread size

Metric threads list diameter in millimeters and pitch in millimeters per thread; UNC lists nominal inch diameter and threads per inch. They are not interchangeable—taps, dies, and gauges differ.

FAQ

What is the standard pitch for an M8 bolt?

The standard pitch for M8 is 1.25 mm. Fine pitch is 1.0 mm.

What drill size should be used for tapping M8 threads?

For M8 x 1.25 coarse thread, use a 6.8 mm tap drill.

What imperial bolt is closest to M8?

5/16-18 UNC is the closest imperial equivalent to M8.
