#8 Screw Size and Thread Specifications

#8 is a common numbered screw size. #8-32 (32 TPI) is widely used in electronics, machinery, and hardware. Diameter is approximately 0.164 in (4.2 mm), close to metric M4.

FAQ

What is #8 screw size?

#8 is a numbered (wire gauge) screw size. #8-32 means a #8 diameter with 32 threads per inch. Diameter is approximately 0.164 in (4.2 mm).

What drill size for #8-32 tap?

Use a #29 drill for #8-32 tapping.

What metric screw is closest to #8?

M4 is the closest metric equivalent to #8. See M4 bolt size for full specs.
