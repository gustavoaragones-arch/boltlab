#10 Screw Size and Thread Specifications

#10 is a common numbered screw size. #10-24 (24 TPI) is used in machinery, automotive, and general hardware. Diameter is approximately 0.190 in (4.8 mm), close to metric M5. Spanish reference: metric & UNC size pages (ES).

FAQ

What is #10 screw size?

#10 is a numbered (wire gauge) screw size. #10-24 means a #10 diameter with 24 threads per inch. Diameter is approximately 0.190 in (4.8 mm).

What drill size for #10-24 tap?

Use a #25 drill for #10-24 tapping.

What metric screw is closest to #10?

M5 is the closest metric equivalent to #10. See M5 bolt size for full specs.
