M12 Bolt Size and Thread Specifications

What is an M12 bolt?

An M12 bolt has 12 mm nominal diameter and 1.75 mm coarse pitch in standard ISO listings. Fine pitch 1.5 mm is common when higher thread engagement is needed in a given length.

M12 is a large metric bolt size used in structural, automotive, and heavy machinery applications. A 12 mm nominal diameter with 1.75 mm pitch provides high strength and is common in flanges and critical joints. Spanish reference: perno M12.

Common uses

M12 bolts are used in structural connections, automotive suspension and drivetrain, machinery flanges, and anywhere high clamp force is required. They are standard in many industrial and automotive specifications.

FAQ

What is the standard pitch for an M12 bolt?

The standard pitch for M12 is 1.75 mm. Fine pitch is 1.5 mm.

What drill size should be used for tapping M12 threads?

For M12 x 1.75 coarse thread, use a 10.2 mm tap drill. Use our Tap Drill Calculator for other pitches.

What imperial bolt is closest to M12?

1/2-13 UNC is the closest imperial equivalent to M12.
